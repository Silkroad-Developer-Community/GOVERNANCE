# Code of Conduct - Silkroad Developer Community

## Standards

This community is dedicated to providing a professional and respectful environment for everyone. We expect all members to adhere to the following standards:

* **Professionalism**: Use welcoming and inclusive language.
* **Respect**: Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
* **Constructive Feedback**: Gracefully accept and provide constructive criticism.
* **Community Focus**: Focus on what is best for the collective community goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment, trolling, or insulting/derogatory comments.
* Personal or political attacks.
* Publishing others' private information (doxing) without explicit permission.
* Any conduct that could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. Instances of unacceptable behavior should be reported to the organization maintainers for review and action.

## Disclaimer

All members must acknowledge that this community is an independent group and is not affiliated with official Silkroad Online developers or publishers. Claiming official status or representation is strictly prohibited.
